About us

Introduction

Tamakoshi Campus was established in 2056 B.S. It was built with intension to provide quality education for students of Khimti, Gelu, Kirney, Debitar, Tilpung, Bhadaurey, Farpu area. The college has met its intensions so far. There are more than 200 students who are currently receiving qulaity education from us. During its 20 plus years journey, it has given many faces enlightment. With every year passing, there has been several improvements in teaching methodologies. Currently Mr. Diwakar Khadka is working as campus chief.
